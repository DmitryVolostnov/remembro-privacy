Remembro App

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 14, 2025  ·  Last updated: April 14, 2025

Remembro is a voice-powered calendar app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

01 What Data We Collect

Remembro may access the following data to provide its core functionality:

  • Voice input — audio is captured and transcribed on-device via Apple's Speech Recognition framework. The resulting text is then sent to Anthropic's API to extract event details (title, date, time). The original audio is never stored or transmitted.
  • Calendar data — event titles, dates, and times created through the app. This data is stored in your personal Apple Calendar (EventKit) and never transmitted to our servers.
  • Device identifiers — anonymous crash and diagnostics data used solely to improve app stability.

02 How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Transcribe your voice into calendar events
  • Add events to your Apple Calendar on your behalf
  • Diagnose crashes and improve app performance

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

03 Microphone & Calendar Access

Remembro requests access to your microphone and calendar. These permissions are used solely within the app to provide the dictation feature. You can revoke permissions at any time via Settings → Privacy on your device.

04 Data Storage & Security

Remembro does not operate its own backend servers. All event data lives in your iCloud / Apple Calendar, protected by Apple's security infrastructure. Voice audio is not recorded or retained after transcription.

05 Third-Party Services

The app may use the following frameworks and services:

  • Speech Framework — for voice-to-text transcription (governed by Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • EventKit — for calendar integration (data stays on your device / iCloud)
  • Anthropic API — the transcribed text of your voice command is sent to Anthropic's servers to extract event details (title, date, time). No audio is transmitted. Governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

06 Children's Privacy

Remembro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

07 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review data stored by the app
  • Delete all calendar events created via Remembro through your native Calendar app
  • Revoke microphone or calendar permissions at any time

08 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
