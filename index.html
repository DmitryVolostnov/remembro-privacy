Privacy Policy

Remembro
Effective date: April 15, 2026
Last updated: April 15, 2026

Remembro is a voice-powered calendar app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when it is shared, and what choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information reasonably needed to operate the app and provide its features.

  • Voice input and transcription. When you use voice input, audio may be processed through Apple's speech technologies to create text. The resulting text may then be sent to Anthropic to interpret your request and extract event details such as title, date, and time.
  • Calendar information. If you grant permission, Remembro can read and write calendar event information needed to create events in your Apple Calendar.
  • Purchase and subscription information. If you purchase a subscription, Apple processes the payment. We may receive limited information from Apple such as subscription status, transaction identifiers, expiration or renewal status, and whether your subscription is active.
  • Diagnostics. We may receive basic crash, error, and performance data to improve reliability.

We do not intentionally collect your full payment card details. Payments are handled by Apple through the App Store.

2. How We Use Information

  • To convert spoken requests into structured event details
  • To create calendar events on your behalf
  • To provide AI-powered request processing
  • To verify and manage access to subscription features
  • To maintain app security, troubleshoot issues, and improve performance

3. Subscription Information

Remembro may offer an auto-renewable subscription priced at approximately USD 0.99 or the local price shown in the App Store. The subscription is billed by Apple, renews automatically unless canceled, and can be managed in your Apple Account settings.

We do not use your subscription information for advertising. We use it only to confirm eligibility for paid features and related app functionality.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only in the following cases:

  • With service providers. For example, Apple for speech and in-app purchase infrastructure, and Anthropic for text processing needed to interpret your requests.
  • For legal reasons. If required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, or security.
  • With your direction. For example, when you allow Remembro to create events in your Apple Calendar.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Apple Speech Framework / Speech Recognition. Used to turn voice input into text.
  • EventKit / Apple Calendar. Used to read and create calendar events on your device or iCloud account, depending on your Apple setup.
  • StoreKit / App Store. Used to process subscriptions and confirm purchase status.
  • Anthropic API. Used to interpret transcribed text and extract structured event details. Audio is not intentionally sent to Anthropic by the app; the app sends text derived from your request.

6. Data Retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, to comply with law, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and maintain the app. Calendar events you create are stored in your Apple Calendar until you delete them. Subscription records may be retained for accounting, fraud prevention, and support purposes.

7. Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect information. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can revoke microphone or calendar permissions in your device settings.
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription in your Apple Account settings.
  • You can delete events created through Remembro from your Calendar app.
  • You can contact us with questions or requests regarding your information.

9. Children's Privacy

Remembro is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. International Processing

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, including where our service providers operate. By using the app, you understand that information may be transferred and processed outside your country, subject to applicable law.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of Remembro after an updated policy becomes effective means you acknowledge the revised policy.
